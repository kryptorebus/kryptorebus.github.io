🧩 Krypto Rebus

Quick Guide to Solving the Rebus Puzzle

🎯 Game Objective

Solve the jackpot rebus by sending the correct amount of ETH that matches a hidden pattern. Each attempt can result in:

  • 🏆 Winning the entire contract balance
  • 💸 Instant return of your deposit – either fixed or random up to 177% of your deposit.
  • 🎁 A clue in the form of a unique NFT — providing a system hint or a more or less clear lead toward the solution

🔁 How to Play

Send ETH to the contract address (via your mobile, wallet or a web interface).

🧠 Game Mechanics

  • 🧩 Clues: Hidden hints are represented by NFTs that lead toward the jackpot solution. Each correct amount sent may yield a clue.
  • 💰 Jackpot: Discover the winning deposit amount — and you win the full contract balance.
  • 🔁 Refunds: Depending on the game settings, your deposit may be refunded partially, in full, or even with a bonus of up to 177%.
  • 🎨 NFTs: Each valid interaction results in minting a custom NFT that may be: a clue, a reward, or a thank-you for participating.
