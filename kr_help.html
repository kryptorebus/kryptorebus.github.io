🧩 Krypto Rebus

Quick Guide to Solving the Rebus Puzzle

🎯 Game Objective

Solve the jackpot rebus by sending the correct amount of ETH (in wei) that matches a hidden pattern. Each attempt can result in:

  • 🏆 Winning the entire contract balance
  • 💸 An immediate refund of your deposit — either fixed or randomized between 70% to 110%
  • 🎁 A clue in the form of a unique NFT — providing a system hint or a more or less clear lead toward the solution

🔁 How to Play

Send ETH to the contract address (via your wallet or a web interface).

🧠 Game Mechanics

  • 🧩 Clues: Hidden hints are represented by NFTs that lead toward the jackpot solution. Each correct amount sent may yield a clue.
  • 💰 Jackpot: Discover the winning deposit amount — and you win the full contract balance.
  • 🔁 Refunds: Depending on game settings, you may get your deposit refunded in part, in full, or even with a bonus.
  • 🎨 NFTs: Each valid interaction results in minting a custom NFT that may be: a clue, a reward, or a thank-you for participating.

📜 Rules

Full game rules are available in the project description.

🛠 Technical Details

  • 🔐 The smart contract is fully on-chain.
  • 💸 Collected fees help grow the jackpot and support project operation.
  • 💾 Winning logic is hash-protected and not publicly visible.

🧪 Tips

  • Every clue leads to another.
  • Clues can be reused and strategically combined to speed up solving.
  • Not winning? It’s likely because:
    • The correct amount hasn’t been discovered yet
    • Or the contract balance isn’t high enough

📍 Example

1. Player A sends 0.005 ETH → receives a clue on how to proceed.
2. Player B sends 0.003 ETH → too small to participate meaningfully.
3. Player A sends 0.007642 ETH → based on the clue, he discovers the solution and wins the jackpot!

🧷 What Do I Need?

  • An Ethereum wallet (e.g. MetaMask)
  • Some ETH to play
  • Curiosity, patience, and a sharp mind 🎲
